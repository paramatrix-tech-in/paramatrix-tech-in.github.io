About Us

At Paramatrix Tech, we specialize in transforming the way power transformer manufacturers design, engineer, and deliver their products. Our intelligent automation solutions convert simple key inputs into complete engineering deliverables—including 2D manufacturing drawings, GA diagrams, 3D models, and BOMs—fully customized to each organization’s workflow and standards.

By eliminating repetitive manual tasks, reducing design errors, and accelerating development cycles, we empower manufacturers to significantly improve efficiency, reduce operational costs, and bring products to market faster than ever before.

Built to integrate seamlessly into existing design ecosystems, our solutions enhance engineering accuracy, consistency, and productivity. With Paramatrix Tech, transformer design becomes smarter, faster, and more reliable—unlocking a new era of digital engineering for the power industry.
